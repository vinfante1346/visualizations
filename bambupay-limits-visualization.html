Important Notes

  • Limits track cumulative activity within rolling time windows (not calendar periods)
  • Cancelled, failed, expired, and deleted transactions are excluded from limits
  • TabaPay may impose additional network-specific limits based on card issuer and user verification level
  • Error code 828 (transfer_velocity) is returned when limits are exceeded
