Key Insights

  • Transnetwork dominates: Controls 60.4% of money movement and 57.6% of transactions. Clear market leader with 10,443 unique users.
  • Uniteller commands premium: Highest average transaction at $221.04, attracting customers sending larger amounts. 35.5% of total volume.
  • Moneygram declining: Only 4.1% of total money movement despite 11.4% of transactions. Significantly lower per-transaction value at $69.13.
  • Engagement varies: Moneygram users most engaged (1.82 tx/account) but smallest user base. Transnetwork balances scale with engagement (1.65 tx/account).
  • Strategic implications: Consider focusing partnership efforts on Transnetwork and Uniteller, which represent 95.9% of total money movement.
