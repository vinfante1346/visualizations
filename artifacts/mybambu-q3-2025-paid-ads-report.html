Paid Ads - Next Steps

The goal is to preserve profitability (low CPA) and algorithmic stability by treating the $200K as a maintenance budget.

1. Strict CPA Focus & Consolidation:

  • Prioritize Low-Funnel: Allocate the majority of the $200K budget to Retargeting/Remarketing and lookalikes of high-intent custom audiences.
  • Channel Concentration: Focus 80%+ of the spend on Google and Meta, the highest-volume platforms with the best recent CPA performance (Sept CPA: Google $39.66, Meta $45.51).

2. Strategic Use of Special Funds (Nov/Dec):

  • tROAS Campaign Execution: Utilize the dedicated $20,000 budget secured for November and December to run tROAS (Target Return On Ad Spend) campaigns in Google.
  • Objective: This investment is crucial for training the Google algorithm for profitability (not just CPA), preparing it for the high-volume spend in January.
